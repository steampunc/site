Know Your Values (Beta Version)

Hi! Do you know what you value? This will hopefully help with that.

Based off of two metrics, it asks you to rank a set of values by your gut "preference". This is a subjective ranking, but it's a subjective problem! Don't feel any pressure to get it exactly right - I hope that through the two methods, you'll get a better sense of what you value.

    Comparing Your Values

    The beta version uses 8 basic values:

    You’ll be shown two values, one on the left side of your screen and one on the right side of your screen. Going largely by your initial reaction, use your keyboard arrow keys to select (i.e., point to) the value that is most important to you.

    If you can’t decide which value is most important, use the down arrow, but whenever possible try and decide between the two.

    If you want to undo your choice, use the up arrow.

    You can also use hjkl bindings if you would prefer.

    The process takes about 5 minutes.

    At the end of the process, we’ll show you your personal value hierarchy and we’ll show you where you are inconsistent.
